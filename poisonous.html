Claim: Some plants are poisonous

Details

Some plants are poisonous. Therefore veganism is unhealthy.

Problems With This Argument

1. This is irrelevant

The problems with this argument should be apparent immediately upon first reading:

  1. You having to eat plants doesn't mean you need to eat every plant equally
  2. Some animals are poisonous too.
  3. Some animals ingest toxins that make it into their products. Example: Abraham Lincoln's mother died from drinking the milk of a cow that had grazed on white snakeroot.

So this is obviously a combination of cherry-picking and non-sequiturs.
