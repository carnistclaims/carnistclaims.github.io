Claim: Some plants are poisonous

Details

Some plants are poisonous. Therefore veganism is unhealthy.

Problems With This Argument

1. Don't like something? Don't eat it

You don't need to eat every plant equally. Even if you have an allergy to a plant doesn't mean you need to take

2. This is actually an argument for veganism

Some animals, like the poison-dart frog ingest toxins that make it into their products. Famously, Abraham Lincoln's mother died from drinking the milk of a cow that had grazed on white snakeroot. So if anything, the existence of toxic plants should make one weary of eating animals that might ingest those plants without your knowledge.
