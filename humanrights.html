Claim: I am only concerned with human rights

Details

The idea here is that the only individuals that deserve rights are humans. The other way this is stated is to simply deny that it is an ethical issue altogether.

Problems with this Argument

1. Almost no carnists actually believe this

We grant plenty of animals rights. Dogs have a right to not be forced into dogfighting. Unless a carnist is willing to step forward and say that they are

2. Animal agriculture hurts people

It destroys ecologies surrounding factory farms, leads to global warming, starves poorer countries of their food, while forcing non-participants to pay for it with animal agriculture subsidies (read: theft), amongst other issues. So yes, it hurts people. You should be concerned with such human rights.
