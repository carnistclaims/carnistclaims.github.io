Claim: Eating Meat helped us evolve higher intelligence

Details

The idea is that eating meat helped us in the past evolutionarily

Problems with this Argument

1. It almost certainly an oversimplification

Our jaws are slimmer and less muscular which allows for greater brain development which facilitated our rise with greater intelligence. What really propelled the human race forward was cooking our food to release the nutrients which requires less chewing, not simply the fact that we ate occasional meat. But that doesn't even matter because...

2. We don't live in the past

What was ethical then isn't now. Xenophobia was a major player in keeping tribes together and facilitating human migration, but does that give us freedom to do whatever we want to people that look different from ourselves? Of course not. If we simply give carte blanche to anything which helps propage the human race, then that would by necessity include rape.

3. It doesn't actually address the argument

You can't respond to the claim that 'carnism hurts animals, other humans, the environment, and yourself' with 'yeah, well it helped us evolve'. That is a complete non-sequitur and does not counter the argument. Carnism remains unethical for those reasons, you've just mentioned an irrelevant fact.
