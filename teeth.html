Claim: We have canines to eat meat

Details

The idea here is that eating meat is ok because we have developed omnivorous canines to do so

Problems with this Argument

1. Yeah... no you do not.

You really think your canines look like an omnivore's? Since bears are such a frequent point of comparison for an omnivorous diet, go ahead and quickly look up bear teeth. Now look in the mirror. Ok, so that wasn't even close. Do the same with racoons.

2. Teeth don't prescribe diet

You can look at the sabre-tooth deer for guidance there if you like. Or gorillas (yes, they are herbivores) if you like. This argument falls apart immediately if literally take 10 seconds to look up any animals ever.

3. Evolution is a bad argument in general

For all of these reasons.
