Claim: 'Free range' or grass-fed meat is sustainable

Details

The carnist here attempts to claim that if everyone ate grass-fed beef it would be more sustainable.

Problems with this Argument

1. Then why do factory farms exist?

If this argument were true, then Factory farms wouldn't exist. Why would you pay someone to truck in soy, corn and grain from other farms when you can let animals openly graze on mostly empty land in the middle of nowhere and essentially feed themselves? It simply isn't true.

2. Greenhouse gas Emissions

Besides issues of overgrazing and reduction in beef supply that would result from a switch to all-grass-fed beef supply, substaintial greenhouse gas emissions would result.

The amazon is already being burned to clear land for animal agriculture. This will additional land use just make it worse.

3. Ethical Implications

This still doesn't address the ethical issues around killing and imprisoning animals that don't wish to die. To the argument that animals are suffering needlessly for your entertainment, you can't simply respond "Yeah, but hey the good news is I found a way to make that more ecologically sustainable!"
