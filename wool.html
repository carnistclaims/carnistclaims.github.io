Claim: Sheep enjoy being sheared

Details

The carnist claim here is that sheep don't get hurt in shearing, so it can't be unethical

Problems With This Argument

1. That's actually not true

Go ahead and look up "mulesing", which is a procedure harmful to the animals. Again, carnists have this idea that it's a cute sheep on a sunny field that enjoys being sheared. This is a fantasy. Sheep are part of animal agriculture, they are in factory farms with the rest of the animals living in confined squalor, and are routinely slashed by automated shears, and are abused and terrorized like all the others once they meet their end.

2. You're still funding animal agriculture

You're still paying money for animal agriculture, and still funding the torture of animals, and therefore are increasing the profitability of taking a sheep and turning it into meat.

Verdict

Wool is animal abuse
