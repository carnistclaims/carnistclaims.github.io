Claim: It's manly to eat meat

Details

Guys imagine themselves a regular Ron Swanson, sitting in an old spice commercial, eating bacon like a real man.

Problems With This Argument

1. You can just buy manliness?

If you can buy manliness at the store and it takes no skill, it's not real manliness. Other people need to do things - hard things - like, for instance, stand their ground on moral principles. I'm glad that taking the easy way out is what real manliness is all about. Go do something impressive.
