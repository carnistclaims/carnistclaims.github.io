Claim: High cholesterol isn't bad for you

Details

Carnists make a variety of claims regarding cholesterol, including the denialism high cholesterol causes heart disease.

Problems With This Argument

1. This has already been fully debunked

These arguments are tired and have been routinely demolished. The TL;DR is they rely on scientific papers with small sample sizes and other methodological problems, or all cite a small handful of authors that essentially republish the same meta analyses and reviews repeatedly in an effort to fake scientific consensus.

The AHA (the multi-billion-dollar medical research organization charged with the CPR certification of essentially every healthcare worker in America) puts it in no uncertain terms on their site that cholesterol, in particular LDL, causes heart disease.
