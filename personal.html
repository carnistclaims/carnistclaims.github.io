Claim: "it's a personal choice"

Details

"I say live and let live; whether or not I eat meat is a personal choice"

Problems With This Argument

1. Every choice you make is technically a personal choice

So, that is a vacuous statement

2. Eating meat affects others

It hurts animals, other people, and the environment. So it technically is no more a personal choice than any other type of immoral activity. Is stealing a personal choice? Is murder? That can't be a serious defense of an accusion that you've harmed a victim in literally any other context.
